Always on the precipice  
of something deep...  

Last night,  
you were hovering over me  
    like a demand  
    to be received,  
        a static feed  
        radioed into the n-degree,  
            but listened here,  
            listened me.  

We're always on the verge  
of something deep...  
    like a vine-ripe gem  
    in the keep.  
        a masterpiece,  
        uncomprehending.  
           a message off the Arecibo  
           dish, collapsed, but ever sending.  

Ohh-sham-jio.  
Ohh-sham-jio  
hauled away.  
    Ohh-sham-jio.  
    Ohh-sham-jio  
    hauled way.  

plunged to the hilt  
pressed to the nines  
sifting the particle fines  
trapped in the sink of my Mponeng tabular mind  

Always on the precipice  
of something deeper...

Last night,  
I was blubbering over you.  
    Minnesota--  
    the brutal winter  
    weather-fend.  
        pissing in the  
        metabolic wind  
            as summers break,  
            as summers bend.  

We're always on the purge  
of something deep  
    to beat a headlong lunge  
    at the gunge.  
         a buried alive  
         on arrival.  
            a tippy-toe annihilation  
            out over your skis   
            at the ballet recital.  

Ohh-sham-jio.  
Ohh-sham-jio  
hauled way.  
    Ohh-sham-jio.  
    Ohh-sham-jio  
    odd to say.  

hack of all trades  
master of numb  
stuck with God's damn in my thumb  
poking rectangulum  
poking the rectangulum  

Always on the precipice   
of something deep...
